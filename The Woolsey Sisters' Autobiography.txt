"The Woolsey Sisters' Autbiography" is the official autobiography of the Woolsey sisters, released in 2008. It covers the whole entire history of Cheryl, Maggie, Lauren, Marie, Texy and Nicole Woolsey and their family and parents from their childhoods all the way through their careers as businesswomen, comediennes, punk rock musicians, actresses, cowgirls and authors. The book also contains illustrations by Lauren and Nicole Woolsey.
