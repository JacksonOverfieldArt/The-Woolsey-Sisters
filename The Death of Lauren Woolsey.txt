Lauren Woolsey, American-British punk rock musician, criminal, animator, artist, and internet personality died of drug overdose in London, England on June 12th, 2016, one year after the death of her boyfriend and Hell Beasts bandmate James Clarke. She was 43 years old when she died. Her five surviving sisters (Cheryl, Maggie, Marie, Texy, and Nicole) and her parents came to England via airplane and visited her funeral. She is currently buried in a London cemetary.
